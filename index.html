the change

We work with our local partners to find the best and most sustainable solutions in each country. These solutions range from improving water quality through household water filters, to boring deep wells, to tapping mountain springs and using gravity to pipe water to an entire community. In every setting, one thing is consistent—we promote sanitation, good hygiene practices and form a local water committee trained to keep the water flowing.
